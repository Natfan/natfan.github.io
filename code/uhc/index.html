High quality UHC games, powered by The Colossal Network

High Powered Servers

We take pride in having some of the best hardware in the business. With 10GB of RAM and an 8 Core i7-4790K Processor, we're able to support over 80 players at once with little lag.

Custom Core Incoming

A team of highly talented developers are working on a custom core with features for both players and staff, letting users have more fun and staff reduce any issues that arise.

Interesting Scenarios

We have the satisfaction of hosting games with a wide variety of scenarios that are different from the rest, such as Risky Retrieval, TripleOres, Barebones and BloodDiamonds.

Different Gamemodes

Try Solo Mode and fight to be the best of the best. Or, if you're feeling more cooperative you could try our Teams modes and fight with your friends to survive your foes!

Get in touch

We love constructive criticism, so please don't hesitate to contact our staff team and tell us what you love, hate or just plain feel ambivalent about! We try to respond to every single message that we get, but try to keep it nice and friendly.
