Overview

Hypixel's UHC Champions is a hardcore survivalist gamemode based around player vs player combat. The game is largely a vanilla gamemode but has the following changes to it.

  • Time is locked at 14:00, or midday. There is no night.
  • Ocean biomes and long River biomes are disabled and will not generate.
    • Interestingly, these biomes will still "spawn", as can be seen in the F3 menu. They will not generate the same way as in vanilla however. This leads to the following changes:
      • River biomes have no water in them, leaving just the river beds.
      • Ocean biomes will generate to have the same terrain structure as Plains biomes.
  • Chickens will always drop at least one Feather.
  • Spiders will always drop at least one String.
  • Sheep will always drop at least one Raw Porkchop.
  • Blazes will always drop at least one Blaze Rod.
  • Ghasts drop one Nether Wart and five Arrows, but no Ghast Tears.
    • This effectively prevents the brewing of Regeneration Potions naturally, although they can still be crafted using custom crafting recipes.
  • Silverfish always drop at least one slimeball (May be false).
  • Skeletons have a 15% chance to drop a full durability bow, along with the vanilla 8.5% chance of dropping a bow with random durability.
  • Wither Skeletons do not inflict the Wither effect on hit.
  • Gravel has a 25% chance to drop flint.
  • All mobs give double the experience.
  • Player kills give an extra 50 EXP.
    • This is equivalent to ~3 levels if you have no XP at all.
  • Blazes randomly spawn in the Nether, you won't need to find a fortress to find Blazes.
  • All players spawn with 40 HP, or 20 Hearts.
  • On death players drop their head, right clicking this head will give 4 seconds of Regeneration II to you and all your teammates.
  • Golden Apples regenerate 4 Hearts instead of 2.
  • Glistening melons require ingots instead of nuggets in their craft.
  • Ore generation is increased.
  • Strength Potions have been nerfed by 62%.
  • Hunger has been nerfed; you won't lose hunger as fast as in vanilla.
  • Each player spawns with a compass pointing at your teammates, within Teams mode.
  • Players get 15 seconds of Fire Resistance upon entering the Nether.
  • Players are given a written book called a "Recipe Book".
    • This will open a GUI when activated.
    • All crafts are in this book, but you may only access recipes for those that you have unlocked.
    • There are multiple pages to this book that can be accessed by clicking the arrows at the bottom of the page.
    • Only custom crafting recipes are available in this book, please see HERE for more information on vanilla crafting recipes.

    The game has two separate modes, Teams and Solo. While generally similar, they have a few key differences between them. These are as follows:

    • Solo has one player per team, Teams has three players per team.
    • Solo has a maximum of 70 players, Teams has a maximum of 111.
    • In Solo, the Golden Nuggets that are gained via the Tomb Robber perk are doubled.
    • In Teams, all players are given a compass that points to their teammates. This is not the same for Solo mode.
      • The focused player can be changed by right clicking.
      • Extra information, such as the focused player's name, distance and health are displayed above the user's hotbar.
