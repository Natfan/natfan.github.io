Life

Hey guys, After reading a long and heartfelt post from a close friend of mine, which had a lot of personal subjects and open thoughts, I decided to document my life on this website. The file structure of /life/x/y, where x is my age as an integer and y is a story from that year. Obviously /life/1 is stories from when I was 1 year old, and /life/99 is stories from when I was 99 years old. That last one hasn't happened yet, PSA. Consider this like the microblogging service Twitter, but it's not micro and it may not even be a blog. Oh, and it ISN'T called Twitter. Yours, - Natfan.
